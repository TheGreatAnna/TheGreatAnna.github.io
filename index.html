Research

My research interests include extremal combinatorics and graph theory. To be specific, I am interested in the following topics
(i) Ramsey theory
(ii) Graph coloring problems
(iii) Turán type problems

Due to my research training experience, I am also interested in the interdisciplinary topics in combinatorics and optimization i.e., combinatorial optimization. One may refer to this page for more information of this field. Interestingly, some graph coloring problems like the classical four color problem can also be viewed as combinatorial optimization problems.
